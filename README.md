# ![App Icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mbliznikova/PomodoroMinimalista/main/assets/app-icon.png) Pomodoro Minimalista

A distraction-free Pomodoro timer for Apple Watch — built for people who procrastinate on their productivity apps.

Live progress ring, haptic alerts, and cross-device sync.


## The Problem
Many productivity apps often include extensive customization, detailed analytics, and streak tracking. For users who need simple focus tools, these additions can create decision fatigue before work even begins, let alone the distractions.

This app takes a different approach: minimal interface, essential functionality only.

## The Solution
A timer designed to get out of your way:
- **One tap to start** — 25-minute default, customizable 1-60 min via Digital Crown
- **Haptic alerts** — works in silent mode, no sound needed
- **Minimal stats** — today's count + overall total (no graphs, no streaks)
- **Cross-device sync** — adjust settings on iPhone, track on Watch

Built for Apple Watch with iOS companion app.
